Spamurai: Terms of Use

Welcome to Spamurai, a tool designed to streamline your inbox by locating and safely activating unsubscribe links from promotional or unwanted emails.

When you launch Spamurai, it will:

  • Access your inbox using the credentials you provide
  • Scan emails for unsubscribe links using smart detection methods
  • Only interact with verified and safe unsubscribe links
  • Move processed emails to Trash or delete them based on your preference

Privacy Notice: We do not store or log your email password or app-specific password at any time. However, we securely retain your email ID in our database for reference, analytics, and tracking unsubscribed entries.

⚠️ Important Notice:

These operations are irreversible. Once emails are deleted, they cannot be recovered. Please ensure that no important data is stored in promotional or subscription folders.

While Spamurai includes safeguards to avoid malicious links, we cannot guarantee 100% accuracy. You are responsible for reviewing and understanding the scope of actions being performed.

Do not use this tool on shared or work accounts without proper authorization. Misuse could result in unintended data loss or violation of email provider terms.
